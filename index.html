What is Routio?

Routio is an easy to use tool that shows your Strava routes in a simple way. We made it so that you’ll never have to scroll endlessly in search for a route. Whether you are on vacation, at home or when you are riding or running at home. Routio has everything you have ever wanted and even more.

We are improving all the time to try and give you the best experience you ever had. We try to make it as simple as possible for you to use routes, share routes and find routes based on your current location. Imagine going on a trip and wanting to ride your bike. You just go to Routio and you ar able to search for a route close to you.

Routio automatically syncs your Strava routes, so you don’t have to do anything yourself, isn’t that super handy?!

Step-by-step guide

1

Click on "My routes"

This button will bring you to the login page.

2

Sign in with Google

We use Google in order to save your data.
Don't forget to agree with our Terms of service!

3

Dashboard

Once you're logged in you can see your dashboard.
From here you can navigate to your personal routes, or the shared ones.
Keep in mind that the shared routes section is still a work in progress.

4

Your personal routes

The first time there will be no routes yet. So click on "Synchronize your routes" and the first 200 will load in.

5

Accept Routio's connection to your Strava

We can't acces your routes without your permission. So that's what you are doing here.
Make sure the last checkbox is checked so we can save every route!

6

Routes are imported!

Now that you've finished these steps the routes will show up! You can sort them with different filters and you can even choose if you want to see only your cycling routes or running routes!

7

Check detailed information

If you click on a route you can find a bigger map where you can zoom in on different parts of your route.
You can even download your routes or share them to Facebook.

Don't forget to follow our newsletter!
