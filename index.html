What is Routio?

Routio is an easy to use tool that shows your Strava routes in a simple way. We made it so that you’ll never have to scroll endlessly in search for a route. Whether you are on vacation, at home or when you are riding or running at home. Routio has everything you have ever wanted and even more.

We are improving all the time to try and give you the best experience you ever had. We try to make it as simple as possible for you to use routes, share routes and find routes based on your current location. Imagine going on a trip and wanting to ride your bike. You just go to Routio and you ar able to search for a route close to you.

Routio automatically syncs your Strava routes, so you don’t have to do anything yourself, isn’t that super handy?!
